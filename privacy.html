Privacy Policy

Effective June 15, 2026

1. Data Collected

The system may collect names, login information and authentication metadata, timestamps, punch records, inventory logs where applicable, QR scans and interactions, device and browser information, and data submitted through system forms.

2. Why Data Is Collected

Data is collected and used for timekeeping, inventory tracking where applicable, operational reporting, business audits, troubleshooting, access control, and system security.

3. No Sale of Data

Personal and operational data collected through the system is not sold.

4. Administrative Review

Authorized administrators may review account activity, punch records, operational records, submissions, and system logs for approved business, reporting, security, and audit purposes.

5. Service Providers

The system may use authentication, database, and hosting providers to operate the software. Those providers process data as needed to provide their services under their applicable terms.

6. Security and Access

Access is limited through account authentication and role-based controls where available. Users are responsible for protecting their login credentials and reporting suspected unauthorized access.
