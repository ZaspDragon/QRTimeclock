QR Time Clock

Employees use their phone to scan, stamp time, and keep lunch records. Managers can review and sign off timesheets.

Eastern Time Not connected

How it works

  1. Employee scans the posted QR code to open this app.
  2. Employee scans their personal QR badge or enters their employee code.
  3. Tap Clock In, Start Lunch, End Lunch, or Clock Out.
  4. Manager logs in, reviews records, and signs completed timesheets.
